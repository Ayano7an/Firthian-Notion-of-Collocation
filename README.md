<h2 id="english">🇺🇸 English</h2>

Unlike traditional 2D graph views, **Knowledge Topology** is an interactive, analytical 3D semantic network designed specifically for the Obsidian Desktop environment. 

It goes beyond mere visual decoration by acting as a powerful retrieval hub: it analyzes the most high-frequency core vocabulary in your vault and renders them into a fluid, interactive sphere equipped with an adaptive physics engine and optical depth-of-field.

### ✨ Core Features & Functionality

* **Interactive Co-occurrence Engine:** Hover over any node in the galaxy, and the engine instantly dims unrelated terms while highlighting "co-occurring words" (words that frequently appear in the same notes). This allows you to visually trace the hidden synapses between your ideas.
* **Deep Contextual Retrieval:** Click on any node to instantly summon a polished modal. It not only lists the notes containing the specific word but also extracts and highlights the exact sentence snippets (context), achieving true zero-friction knowledge tracing.
* **Global Relative Viewport Scaling:** Equipped with a hardcore responsive algorithm. Whether you collapse your sidebar to its narrowest limit or scale up your Obsidian UI, the 3D sphere, font sizes, and layout constraints scale perfectly in relative proportion, avoiding any overlapping or graphical crushing.
* **Optical Depth-of-Field (DoF) Aesthetics:** Implements a cinematic progressive blur algorithm. Nodes rotating to the back of the sphere exponentially fade and blur, creating a clean, un-cluttered frontal view with an immersive sense of 3D space.

<h2 id="简体中文">🇨🇳 简体中文</h2>

与传统的 2D 关系图谱不同，**Knowledge Topology (知识拓扑网络)** 是一套专为 Obsidian 桌面端打造的**交互式 3D 语义分析中枢**。

它绝非单纯的视觉装饰，而是具备强大检索能力的生产力工具：通过底层分词引擎提纯你知识库中的核心高频词汇，并利用带有光学景深与自适应物理算法的 3D 引擎进行流体渲染。

### ✨ 核心功能与技术特性

* **共现语义追踪 (Co-occurrence Analysis)：** 将鼠标悬停在星系中的任意节点上，引擎会瞬间暗化无关词汇，并高亮显示与该词“在同一篇笔记中共同出现过”的关联词汇。让你直观地看见知识节点之间隐秘的思维突触。
* **上下文极速穿透 (Contextual Retrieval)：** 点击任意词汇节点，将瞬间唤出极具设计感的溯源面板。不仅展示包含该词的笔记列表，更会直接提取并高亮该词在文章中的“原话片段（Snippets）”，实现零阻力知识回溯。
* **全局相对论缩放算法 (Adaptive Viewport Scaling)：** 内置极其强悍的响应式渲染管线。无论你将 Obsidian 侧边栏拉到极窄，还是大幅调整了 UI 缩放比例，3D 球体的半径、字体大小、文字间距都会进行极其精准的**等比相对缩放**，绝对不会出现文字挤压或排版崩坏。
* **光学级浅景深视觉 (Optical DoF Aesthetics)：** 引入电影级的渐进式高斯模糊算法。转动到球体后半部分的节点会呈现指数级的透明度衰减与物理失焦，极大拉开前后景的体积反差，彻底告别文字重叠带来的视觉噪音。
